About Us

Andra Spice is a small restaurant near BMS College. A typical Andhra restaurant serving meals to college students and nearby employees, this restaurant has some decent non veg items. The Biriyani here is a little spicy, going by its Andhra nature. The food here is served on the plantain leaves thus making it a nice foody affair.
